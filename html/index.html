Our Manufacturing Partners

QOVANS INDUSTRIE

Founded in Normandy in 1964, Qovans brings more than 55 years of success in the design, production and marketing of removable office systems and solutions for the development of workpads and tertiary spaces.

Our headquarters as well as our industrial site with an area of 35,000 m² is in Falaise in Calvados. Qovans is positioned on the market as a solid group, a reference in the sector. We are a constantly evolving company thanks to its projects and through our Clips®, P85®, Saniclips® and Fontex® brands which claim MADE IN FRANCE.

Rue de l'Industrie, 14700 Falaise, France

www.qovans.com

Gerflor Group

For over 80 years, the Gerflor Group has been designing, manufacturing and marketing innovative, decorative solutions with a focus on environmental and social responsibility.

Its product portfolio includes resilient flooring, wall coverings and finishing elements. Gerflor provides comprehensive solutions for commercial, healthcare, education, retail, transport, and office environments.

www.gerflorgroup.com
